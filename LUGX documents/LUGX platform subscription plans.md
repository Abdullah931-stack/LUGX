Subscription options on the "LUGX" platform are distributed across three strategic categories, meticulously designed to meet diverse user requirements and provide integrated technical solutions tailored to various usage levels:

**Free Plan:**
This category serves as the default starting point available to all users upon completing the registration process. The plan grants a weekly usage capacity of 2,000 words, consumed across Correct, Improve, and Translate services. Regarding summarization tools, the plan allows processing one text per day with a maximum of 500 words, while the "ToPrompt" feature remains entirely deactivated at this level, with its icon hidden from the user interface.

**PRO Plan:**
This category represents the advanced level and is the "Recommended" choice for elevating performance efficiency. The plan is available at an exclusive cost of $12 (instead of $15) after applying a limited-time 20% discount. It provides broader operational limits, allowing for the processing of 20,000 words per day for Correct, Improve, and Translate services combined. It also raises the summarization feature ceiling to accommodate texts up to 5,000 words per process, at a rate of 5 uses per day, in addition to enabling the generation of 10 commands daily via the "ToPrompt" feature. The benefits of this upgrade are not limited to quantitative expansion but also extend to enhancing output quality by employing more powerful and professional language models.

**Ultra Plan:**
Classified as the most powerful and efficient plan available, it is designed for intensive use requiring exceptional performance. The plan is available for $120 (instead of $160) after applying a 25% discount. This category grants superior operational limits of up to 250,000 words per day for Correct, Improve, and Translate operations. Regarding the summarization feature, it supports processing massive texts up to 30,000 words at a time, with a daily usage limit of 50 times. It also provides extensive production capacity for the "ToPrompt" feature at 500 commands per day. This plan ensures an integrated user experience based on the finest technical models to deliver results characterized by the highest degrees of accuracy and professionalism.
